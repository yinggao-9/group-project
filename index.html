Our Story

About Us

In 1980, three visionary founders—Evelyn Lizeth, Joseph Munene Mbogori, and Ying Gao—came together in Ireland to establish Adlon Hotel. Their vision was simple yet profound: to create a unique space where guests could experience both the warmth and comfort of home, alongside exceptional professional service.

For over four decades, We have received numerous industry accolades—from "Best Global Boutique Hotel" to "Excellence in Service Award"—but what we treasure most is the genuine praise we've earned from guests around the world. Countless travelers have found a "home away from home" within our walls, and have carried our story to every corner of the globe.

From 3 to 72—and growing with heart.

Our journey began with three founders. Today, our Adlon family includes 72 dedicated professionals. But what truly sets us apart is not our size—it's the sincerity, passion, and excellence that every team member brings. We remain committed to delivering nothing less than the finest service, ensuring every guest feels the warmth of our care.
